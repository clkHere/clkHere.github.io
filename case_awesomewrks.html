Background

AdventureWorks, Inc. is a multinational manufacturer and seller of bicycles and bicycle accessories. The sales manager expressed frustration with static sales reports. There was an immediate need to have accessible and up-to-date executive sales reports in order to rapidly assess and adjust sales strategy.

Business Request & User Stories

The sales manager - Stephen Marshall - would like to improve sales reports by switching from static reports to more dynamic ones in the form of visual dashboards.
